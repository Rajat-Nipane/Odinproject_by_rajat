Description

Omlette is made from eggs, we take the yolk and white part mix it properly with other ingredients and fry it.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • oil
  • Onions
  • Green Chillies
  • Coriander
  • Tomato

Steps:

  1. Take Eggs and took out the yolk and white part in container
  2. Chop onions,tomato, chillies and coriander.
  3. Mix everything and give a whisk and add spices according to you
  4. Put everything on pan and keep it until it becomes golden brown
  5. Flip it and add cheese if you want
  6. Enjoy

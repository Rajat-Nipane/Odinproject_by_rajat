Description

Noodles are a type of food made from unleavened dough which is rolled flat and cut, stretched or extruded, into long strips or strings. Noodles can be refrigerated for short-term storage or dried and stored for future use.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • oil
  • Onions
  • Green Chillies
  • Veggies

Steps:

  1. Boil Noodles
  2. Prepare the veggies
  3. Drain noodles
  4. Fry veggies with onion, green chillies and garlic
  5. Once cooked , put noodles and sauces.
  6. Mix them properly and tada noodles is ready.

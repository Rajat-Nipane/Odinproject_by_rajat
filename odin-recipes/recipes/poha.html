Description

Poha is flattened rice cooked with fried onions, chillies, potato and cumins.It can be topped with coriander, peanuts and lemon.

Ingredients

  • Flatened Rice
  • Onions
  • Potato
  • Green Chillies
  • Peanuts
  • Coriander
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Soak the flattened rice in water for 5 minutes
  2. Chops onions and green chillies length wise
  3. Take a fry pan and put some vegetable oil in that
  4. Now fry onion,potato and green chillies for 3-4 minutes and then put tumeric masala etc
  5. Add Flattend Rice on that and sprinkle coriander
  6. Add "Tarri" and present it
